Video games are important :3


The importance of video games is under investigation and analysis, however it has been established that video games play an important role in shaping personality and intelligence, as long as they are used for this purpose.


The term 'indie' or independent is used to refer to 'small' games programmed, managed and produced by their authors with their own capital or by other means such as user donations and the help of the gaming community.


In Frog Reviews you will get to know some of these wonderful games and maybe you will get involved in this peculiar world of indie games. :3
